Dog Training

Dog training results are based on owner's own application of techniques learned with Goody Four Paws. Training is not an exact science and takes time to achieve good results. Dog training has better results with young puppies than with adult dogs, so we recommend starting early and sticking to a very strict routine. Not to say that training adult dogs isn't possible but it takes a bit longer to have the desired results. We will work together and make your pooch a Goody Four Paws in no time! To start a training plan, we will need a list of what you need to fix immediately and what you would like to work on. We can assist with toilet training, recall, fetch, biting, chewing, lead pulling, resistance to walking on a leash, and more. We must emphasise that training your dog takes months, and the real work lies with you. We provide you with the tools and the language your dog will understand to reinforce them, improve your relationship and ensure manageable and enjoyable behaviour for years to come. While we can usually offer assistance in one or two sessions, we also provide a 10-session plan, as training requires commitment, and we are dedicated to helping you achieve the best possible results.
